About Parwad

A scenic village in Belagav district known for its seasonal waterfall, warm community, and local festivals.

Parwad is a small village celebrated for its natural beauty and cultural traditions. Locals host vibrant festivals, maintain historic temples, and the surrounding landscape becomes lush and photogenic especially after the monsoon.

  • Highlights: Waterfall, Temple, Local festivals
  • Best time to visit: Post-monsoon (when waterfall is at its fullest)

Culture & Attractions

Parwad’s community life includes festivals, temple fairs, and traditions handed down through generations.

Waterfall

A scenic waterfall close to the village — beautiful for day trips and photos during/after monsoon.

Temple

Local temple with traditional architecture; the center of many community events and rituals.

Festivals

Colorful festivals, folk music, and local cuisine connect residents and visitors alike.
